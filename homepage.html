What Should I Be Working On?

This week is wrap-up week for ONEXYS. We're making available the post-test via a link at the top of the this page (see new link above the progress bar). This is a great way to measure your progress this summer in addition to the fact that it's part of what we're expecting you to do to complete ONEXYS. It's very much of the same style/flavor/vibe as the diagnostic test you took before ONEXYS begins. Please complete the diagnostic by 11:59p August 15th (EDT). After you are done, your coach will meet with you to conclude the summer.

If you still need some more time to finish other ONEXYS activities, we're going to keep things open for another couple of days. We hope you have a great week and are looking forward to seeing you on campus on August 31st at our ONEXYS Shake Shack part. More details to come on this...

Jaihui talks about the Office of Career Strategy, Kenneth talks about moving day (right around the corner!), Isaiah talks about office hours, Ally and Seewan talk about writing tutors, and James Kim talks about the Tutoring Website, and Alicia talks about Science Hill. Check out all of these here! Keep working - you're almost done!
